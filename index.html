I'm a civil engineering graduate turned software engineer. I studied civil engineering because I have a passion for numbers and the logical processes involved in thorough analysis and practical problem-solving. My attraction to software engineering originated with videogames... I really like the ability to create digital experiences that transcend cultural and national boundaries.

I still have fond memories of using my family's old IBM AT, loading up BASIC, and typing PRINT "Zaid" for the first time. The feeling of testing out simple commands from the BASIC User's Guide was exhilarating, being able to exert complete control over the device by typing in a few symbols, limited only by my inventiveness and creativity.

I rediscovered my passion for computers and software engineering in 2018 after using MATLAB in my Structural Analysis class. Since then, I've completed numerous computer science and software development courses, learning languages such as Python, C, C++, JavaScript and web development frameworks such as Django, NodeJS, and Flask. I've also worked on personal and professional projects, from a Twitter clone, to a stock trading web application, to a car rental back-end/API.
